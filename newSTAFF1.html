Sharif Bey opens at Carnegie Museum of Art

Pittsburgh-born Sharif Bey, a proud graduate of Saturday Art Connection classes at Carnegie Museum of Art, loved hanging out at both Oakland museums as a young “art kid.” In Oct. 2021, CMOA launched an exhibition inspired by his “excavations” of the museum collections, titled Sharif Bey: Excavations. KDKA’s Kristine Sorenson spoke with Sharif Bey, walking with him through the exhibition. Read more from WESA and the Pittsburgh Post-Gazette.

Photo: Bryan Conley

Enthusiastic response to CMP’s Community Access Memberships

At the close of 2021, more than 3,100 teens had signed up for Carnegie Museums’ free Teen Memberships; more than 500 families or individuals had become Family Access Members at the greatly reduced price of $20; and 30 organizations had signed up for free Organizational Outreach Memberships. The new Community Access Memberships launched on Oct. 27. Read what the Pittsburgh Post-Gazette editorial board had to say about them.
